Develop specialist skills with an accredited Psychology degree

Our BSc (Hons) Psychology degree offers an ideal foundation if you are aiming to become a practising psychologist or for other employment routes including human resources, marketing, research or media and communications. It will provide you with a sound foundation in all the key areas of psychology and specialist fields including cognitive, biological, developmental, clinical, health, forensic psychology and neuroscience.

The programme is accredited by the British Psychological Society (BPS) and provides eligibility for Graduate Basis for Chartered Membership (GBC).

Why study your BSc (Hons) Psychology with us?

  • Our BSc (Hons) Psychology programme is accredited by the British Psychological Society (BPS)
  • You will develop professional, intellectual, methodical and practical skills through workshops
  • This programme prepares you for employment, future study, or training for professional practice
  • You will benefit from career-planning workshops and the opportunity to apply for a research internship in the department’s research projects

BPP University: Part of the BPP Professional Education Group

BPP Professional Education Group is one of Europe’s largest, specialist, professional education providers – established for over 40 years as a provider of accountancy, financial and legal qualifications. Our Pathways into higher education

If you do not meet the entry requirements for our BSc (Hons) Psychology programme:

Our Pathway to Higher Education Certificate in Business Management gives you the opportunity to develop the knowledge, skills and confidence to succeed in your undergraduate degree with us. Plus, you are guaranteed a place on the BSc (Hons) Psychology degree upon successful completion of your Pathway.
