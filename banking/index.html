Study an accredited Banking degree taught in the heart of London City

Our BSc (Hons) Banking and Finance degree is designed to help you develop the desirable skills and knowledge required for a successful career in the financial sector.

As the programme is also integrated with various professional qualifications, it provides you with the teaching required to undertake external professionally recognised exams, such as the Investment Management Certificate (IMC), Investment Advice Diploma and Chartered Financial Analyst – CFA Level I. There is also a route which leads to Chartered Banker Accreditation.

Why study your BSc (Hons) Banking & Finance with us?

  • We are an Investment Management Certificate Advantage Partner (IMC) and have an outstanding reputation with professional bodies in the sector
  • Study at our Business School in the heart of the City of the London – UK’s largest financial centre
  • You can choose from elective modules which will prepare you for prestigious professional qualifications: Investment Management Certificate (IMC), Investment Advice Diploma (IAD) and Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Level I.
  • The programme offers the choice to graduate in as little as two years full-time with the accelerated programme

BPP University: Part of the BPP Professional Education Group

BPP Professional Education Group is one of Europe’s largest, specialist, professional education providers – established for over 40 years as a provider of accountancy, financial and legal qualifications. The Group delivers programmes across the UK, internationally and through our innovative online learning platforms. BPP University is a distinct legal entity of BPP Professional Education Group, with its own degree-awarding powers granted by the UK Privy Council.

We are dedicated to preparing students for their professional career. We offer a strong commercial approach, within a business culture designed to help students stand out in the workplace after they graduate. Our programmes are designed in partnership with employers and respected professionals in the fields of law, business, finance and health.
