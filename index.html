Get more than just a qualification. Join a profession.

At BPP University, we are the experts the experts turn to; we actively shape and deliver the next generation of professionals through our academically rigorous programmes. Much of our programme content is designed in collaboration with businesses and employers and you will be taught in professional workspaces by tutors with practitioner experience. Everything is aimed at getting you ready for a rewarding career in your chosen profession.

Within BPP University Law School, the trainees 60 leading UK and global law firms study exclusively with us, including Reed Smith and DWF.

Committed to providing an outstanding student experience

You will learn in professional study spaces with modern resources and technology. We take a practical approach to your studies, helping you to transform theory into practice to thoroughly prepare you for your chosen career.

Our various study centres are equipped with mock courtrooms for LLB (Hons) programmes, clinic spaces for our health students and those studying business or finance thrive learning in an innovative office space situated in the heart of London City.

BPP University Business School programmes have been successfully delivered to leading companies such as Lloyds Banking Group and TUI.

Your tutors are professionals

We pride ourselves on our tutors’ invaluable combination of practical and academic experience. They are qualified accountants, bankers, managers, barristers, solicitors, psychologists, nurses, chiropractors and other health professionals.

They bring this first-hand experience to your classroom so you can translate your studies into practice. Our tutors truly understand the profession you wish to join as they are part of it themselves.

Our teaching quality is of the highest standard. In the most recent survey by Legal Week, BPP University Law School was ranked in the top five* UK universities for quality of teaching.*

* Source: Legal Week, 2014

Programmes designed around you

As well as the standard three-year degree, we also provide accelerated degrees over two years to reduce the time you invest and get you into the workplace sooner.

We build ﬂexibility into our learning programmes from the beginning - to allow you to build your studies around other demands on your time. We also oﬀer up to three diﬀerent start dates each year.

We educate students from over half the world’s countries; creating a diverse and multi-cultural study environment.

Our locations

BBP University’s undergraduate programmes are ran out of modern study centres located throughout England. All of our study centres are located near centres of business, finance, law or healthcare – giving you close contact with many of the businesses and institutions that set the standard for graduate recruitment.

If you choose to study law, you’ll be able to study in either London or Leeds; Business and Finance degrees are delivered from our Business School based in the heart of London City; and our Health programmes can be studied in London or Manchester centres.
