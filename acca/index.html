We offer multiple programmes to help you become an ACCA exam qualified accountant

The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) professional accounting qualification is globally-recognised. BPP University's Advanced Diploma in Accounting and Finance [ACCA route] programmes is fully accredited by the ACCA and has been designed to provide you with core knowledge in key areas to ensure that you have the skills to go on to complete the qualification with ACCA.

Why should you study your Advanced Diploma in Accounting and Finance [ACCA route] with us?

  • This programme is fully accredited by the ACCA on a module-by-module basis and mirrors the ACCA Fundamentals level structure and syllabus
  • BPP University will manage all your assessments and exemptions from the ACCA F1 to F9 papers
  • Learning materials are provided by BPP Learning Media, an ACCA-approved provider for content
  • You will receive professionally-orientated tuition from qualified practitioners
