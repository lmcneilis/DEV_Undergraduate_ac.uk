Join a world-renowned Law School

If you want to be the best, you need to learn from the best. With over 20 years’ experience in delivering professional legal qualifications, we are now one of the largest law schools in the UK.

As testimony to this, we are trusted by 60 international and global law firms who send their trainee solicitors to study with us. We pride ourselves on building and shaping legal careers by developing our students into focused and highly employable solicitors or barristers.

Why study your LLB (Hons) or LLB (Hons) International Commercial Law with us?

  • You will join a world-renowned law school with exclusive, global law firm clients
  • It is a practical law degree designed to meet employer needs and taught by experienced legal professionals and academics
  • You may study specialised electives from all areas and types of practice including: Family Law, Intellectual Property Law, Medical Law, Employment Law and Company Law
  • You can, subject to BPP University’s general eligibility and admission criteria, secure a place on our LPC or MA (LPC) with Business if you obtain a minimum 2.2 on our LLB (Hons) degree

BPP University: Part of the BPP Professional Education Group

BPP Professional Education Group is one of Europe’s largest, specialist, professional education providers – established for over 40 years as a provider of accountancy, financial and legal qualifications. The group delivers programmes across the UK, internationally and through our innovative online learning platforms. BPP University is a distinct legal entity of BPP Professional Education Group, with its own degree-awarding powers granted by the UK Privy Council.

We are dedicated to preparing students for their professional career. We offer a strong commercial approach, within a business culture designed to help students stand out in the workplace after they graduate. Our programmes are designed in partnership with employers and respected professionals in the fields of law, business, finance and health.

Our Pathways into higher education

If you do not meet the entry requirements for our LLB programmes:

Our Pathway to Higher Education Certificate in Legal Studies gives you the opportunity to develop the knowledge, skills and confidence to succeed in your undergraduate degree with us. Plus, you are guaranteed a place on our LLB programmes upon successful completion of the Pathway.
